About

Kamolon osh center:

Kamolon Osh Markazi is one of the most popular places to eat in the Republic of Uzbekistan. This center has been known for many years for its excellent cuisine and welcoming culture. Osh has a special place among cooking centers and is a popular place for eating.

History of Kamolon:

Kamolon Osh Markazi was founded in the 1990s, and it started as a small restaurant. Over time, the center expanded its activities and became a large and modern cooking center. Currently, the center can accommodate hundreds of customers.

Environment and Design:

Kamolon cooking center is distinguished by its combination of traditional and modern styles. The interior design is decorated in a national style, enriched with national motifs and ornaments. Here you will feel at home and you can experience our national culture.

Meals and Services:

The menu of Kamolon osh center is very wide and varied. Here you can try the following dishes: Pilaf: Uzbek national dish, made from rice, meat, vegetables and spices. Somsa: A doughy meat or vegetable pie, baked in the oven. Manti: Meat and onions are placed in the dough and steamed. Lag'mon: A meat and vegetable soup made with special hand-pulled noodles.
